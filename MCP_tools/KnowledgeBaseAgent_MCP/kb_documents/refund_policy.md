# Refund Policy (Sample)

This document is sample internal knowledge for testing the Knowledge Base Agent.

## Standard refunds

- **Within 14 days** of purchase: full refund to the original payment method.
- **15–30 days**: store credit only, unless the product is defective.
- **After 30 days**: no refund except where required by law or for defective items under warranty.

## Enterprise customers

Enterprise contracts follow the **Master Service Agreement (MSA)**. Refunds and credits are handled per Schedule B unless the account manager approves an exception.

## How to request a refund

1. Customer opens a ticket in the support portal with order ID.
2. Support verifies eligibility using this policy and order history.
3. Finance processes approved refunds within **5 business days**.
